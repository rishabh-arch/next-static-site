How to Use Youtube Media?
  • Find a video you want in the playlist.
  • Under the video, click Save.
  • Select a playlist you've already created, or click Create new playlist +
  • If you create a playlist, enter a playlist name.
  • Use the box to select your playlist's privacy setting. If it's private, only you can view the playlist.
  • Click Create.
  • For PC
    • Open your Playlist.
    • Copy the url from the address bar.
    • Url must look like this https://www.youtube.com/ watch?v=q6EoRBvdVPQ&list=PLZ4DbyIWUw Cq4V8bIEa 8jm2ozHZVuREJP
    • then finally paste it to your media.
  • For Mobile
    • Open your Playlist.
    • Click on the three dots on the top right corner.
    • Click on share.
    • Click on Copy link.
    • then finally paste it to your media.
